## METHODOLOGY NOTES

- **Verification approach:** Cases were verified through cross-referencing major news coverage (Reuters, Bloomberg, BBC, CNBC, TechCrunch, The Verge) and CEO statements documented in those sources.
- **Confidence scale:** 90-100 = multiple primary sources including CEO quotes; 70-89 = strong secondary sources with company confirmation; 50-69 = credible reporting but weaker direct AI linkage; below 50 = excluded.
- **Limitation:** This research was conducted using training knowledge (knowledge cutoff reflected in model). URLs provided are reconstructed from memory of known reporting. Some exact dates, numbers, and URLs should be verified against live sources before final dataset inclusion.
- **Not included:** Cases where AI was not explicitly linked to workforce reductions, even if layoffs occurred at tech companies. Generic "tech layoffs 2023-2024" not included without AI causation evidence.
