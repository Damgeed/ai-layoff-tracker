Entries under review or with challenged claims. The AI Layoff Tracker maintains a transparent dispute log so researchers and journalists can assess the reliability of every data point.

About This Page

This page lists entries in the AI Layoff Tracker database that have received formal dispute submissions. A dispute may be filed when an external party — such as a company representative, a researcher, or a journalist — challenges the accuracy or classification of an entry. Disputes are reviewed against the same evidentiary standards as our corrections process.

Each dispute is logged with the company name, classification, the specific reason for the challenge, the current review status, and the date the dispute was filed. Resolved disputes include a link to the outcome.

Dispute Process

  1. Submission. A dispute is submitted via email to corrections@ailayofftracker.com with supporting evidence.
  2. Acknowledgment. The submitter receives confirmation within 72 hours, along with a tracking identifier.
  3. Review. Our team evaluates the dispute against the original source material and any newly provided evidence. Classification is reassessed if the dispute concerns tier assignment.
  4. Resolution. The dispute is either accepted (entry updated), rejected (entry unchanged, with explanation), or held pending additional evidence. All resolutions are published in the changelog.
