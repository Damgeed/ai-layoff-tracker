How the Review Queue Works

Before any workforce reduction event is added to the AI Layoff Tracker, it passes through a verification pipeline. Submissions are held here until they meet all inclusion criteria: a public source, a documented workforce reduction, explicit AI attribution, and a verifiable date.

The review process follows the six-step verification protocol described in our methodology. Entries that fail review are rejected with an explanation. Those that pass are published to the dataset and removed from this queue.

Submit an Entry

If you have evidence of a workforce reduction linked to AI that is not yet in the database, you can submit it for review. Submissions must include:

  • Company name — the organization that made the reduction.
  • Date — when the reduction was announced or implemented (at minimum, month and year).
  • Jobs affected — the number of positions eliminated, if known.
  • Source URL — a publicly accessible link to the primary source (news article, company press release, SEC filing, etc.).
  • AI connection — a brief explanation of how AI was cited as a factor.

Send submissions to submit@ailayofftracker.com. All submissions are reviewed by a human; no automated ingestion is used.
