Stack Overflow laid off approximately 100 employees (18% of workforce) following a significant decline in traffic after the launch of ChatGPT and GitHub Copilot, as developers increasingly turned to AI for coding answers instead of the Q&A platform.

Evidence

  • Company Statement: Company confirmed traffic decline from AI tools necessitated workforce reduction.
  • Traffic Analysis: Third-party traffic analysis confirmed 35%+ decline in Stack Overflow page views since ChatGPT launch.
