📅 2024-05-20 📍 United States 🏭 Technology

IBM announced plans to pause hiring for roles it believes AI can perform, affecting approximately 7,800 back-office positions. CEO Arvind Krishna stated AI will replace many non-customer-facing roles over the next five years.

"I could easily see 30 percent of [back-office roles] getting replaced by AI and automation over a five-year period."

— IBM CEO
