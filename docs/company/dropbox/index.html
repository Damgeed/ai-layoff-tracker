📅 2023-04-27 📍 San Francisco, California, USA 🏭 Technology 📊 Public

Dropbox laid off 500 employees (16% of workforce) as part of a restructuring to focus on AI-powered products. CEO Drew Houston stated the company needed to redirect resources toward 'the next stage of growth driven by AI.'

"In an ideal world, we'd simply shift people from one team to another. And we've done that wherever possible. However, our next stage of growth requires a different mix of skill sets, particularly in AI and early-stage product development."

— Dropbox CEO
