📅 2024-06-17 📍 Santa Clara, California, USA 🏭 Education Technology 📊 Public

Chegg laid off 441 employees (20% of workforce) after ChatGPT's launch significantly reduced demand for its homework help and tutoring services. CEO Dan Rosensweig directly attributed the revenue decline to students shifting to free AI tools.

"Since March we saw a significant spike in student interest in ChatGPT. We believe it's having an impact on our new customer growth rate."

— Chegg CEO
